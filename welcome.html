High-Intensity Interval Training

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a time-efficient workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief periods of rest. This approach maximizes calorie burn and improves cardiovascular fitness, making HIIT a popular choice for those seeking to achieve results quickly.
